# NapCEO — Executive Nap Management Platform

## Vision
NapCEO is the world's first enterprise-grade nap optimization platform. We're disrupting the $432B sleep industry by bringing agile methodology to unconsciousness.

## The Problem
- 73% of workers nap inefficiently with no KPIs or sprint planning
- Zero visibility into nap ROI across organizations
- No way to benchmark your napping against industry peers
- Nap debt is an untracked liability on every company's balance sheet

## The Solution
A mobile app that treats napping like a Fortune 500 company treats quarterly earnings.

### Core Features

**Nap Portfolio Management**
Track your nap assets across multiple categories: Power Nap (15 min), Strategic Siesta (30 min), Executive Recharge (45 min), and Board-Level Blackout (60+ min). View historical nap performance with candlestick charts.

**Sleep Equity**
Earn Sleep Equity (SLP) tokens for consistent napping. Vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. Trade on the NapExchange with other nappers. Current valuation: $0.00003 per SLP.

**Quarterly Nap Reviews**
AI-generated performance reviews for your napping. "Your Q2 nap output exceeded expectations, but your REM-to-light-sleep ratio needs improvement. Consider pivoting to a more aggressive siesta strategy."

**Chief Nap Officer Title Progression**
Start as Nap Intern. Progress through Nap Associate, Senior Napper, VP of Unconsciousness, SVP of Horizontal Strategy, and finally: Chief Nap Officer (CNO). Title unlocks at 10,000 lifetime nap minutes.

**Nap Standup Meetings**
Daily 30-second audio summaries of yesterday's napping. "Good morning team. Yesterday I executed a 23-minute power nap at 14:07 with a sleep efficiency of 87%. Blockers: the neighbor's dog."

**IPO Your Nap Schedule**
Once you hit CNO status, you can IPO your nap schedule. Other users can invest in your napping potential. If you nap well, investors profit. If you pull an all-nighter, the stock crashes.

## Target Market
- Burnt-out tech workers (TAM: everyone)
- Corporate middle managers who already nap at their desk but lack the data to prove ROI
- College students who want to professionalize their lifestyle
- Anyone who has ever said "I'll just rest my eyes for a minute"

## Business Model
- Freemium: Track up to 3 naps/day
- NapCEO Pro ($9.99/mo): Unlimited naps, AI nap coaching, custom sleep sounds (VC pitch deck white noise, earnings call ASMR)
- NapCEO Enterprise ($49/seat/mo): Team nap analytics, org-wide nap OKRs, Slack integration ("Eric is in a Sprint Nap. ETA: 14:32")

## Metrics That Matter
- DAU (Daily Active Unconsciousness)
- NPS (Nap Promoter Score)
- ARR (Annual Recurring Rest)
- CAC (Cost of Acquiring Consciousness)
- LTV (Lifetime Total Zzz's)

## Competitive Landscape
- Sleep Cycle: Tracks sleep but doesn't gamify it. No IPO feature. Boring.
- Headspace: Meditation, not napping. Fundamentally different paradigm.
- Calm: Nice sounds but no equity compensation for sleeping.
- A literal pillow: Our strongest competitor. Zero tech debt. Perfect UX.
